NLMK has two service centres operating in the Americas – NLMK North America Plate LLC and NLMK South America Sales Pte. Ltd. – as well as a trade office and a logistics office in Brazil.

NLMK’s Brazilian representative offices offer premium steel products such as Quard abrasion resistant steel and Quend high yield strength structural steel, serving manufacturers of construction and mining equipment.

The North and South America account for over 7% of NLMK Group’s total sales.
