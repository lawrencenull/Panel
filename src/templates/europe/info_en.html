NLMK Europe

NLMK Europe produces a wide range of HVA flats. NLMK’s industrial model is unique in Europe. It is based on the efficient supply of semi-finished products (slabs) from Russia to flexible, high-quality European processing facilities close to key customers.

NLMK Europe includes two business units: NLMK Europe Strip and NLMK Europe Plate.
