NLMK USA

LMK USA produces flat steel of the highest quality. There are three companies in the division, electric arc furnace (EAF) and hot-rolling facilities, and a common distribution structure.

Unlike other large steel companies that offer a standard range of products, NLMK USA’s plants can work individually with each customer to develop slabs with new chemical compositions.

  • 700,000 tonnes of steel and 2 million tonnes of steel products a year
  • NLMK USA is one of the top five steelmakers in the US
  • NLMK USA accounts for 2% of the nation’s total steel production
  • Every year, more than 200 customers and their partners receive training through the "Physical & Process Metallurgy Fundamentals’" course provided by NLMK USA in cooperation with local universities
